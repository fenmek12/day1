Great White Shark

The great white shark is one of the largest predatory fish in the ocean. It is known for its speed, power, and sharp teeth, making it an apex predator in marine ecosystems.

Interesting Facts

  • Great white sharks can grow up to 20 feet long.
  • They can detect a single drop of blood in 25 gallons of water.
  • They have been around for over 400 million years.
