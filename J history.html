Historical Timeline

  1. Adwa

    Adwa's was most notably the site of the final battle of the First Italo-Ethiopian War, where the Ethiopian Emperor Menelik II fought to defend Ethiopia's independence against Italy in 1896. The town and its environs suffered considerable damage during the fighting.

  2. Hiroshima

    On August 6, 1945, the United States dropped an atomic bomb on the city of Hiroshima. The bomb was known as “Little Boy”, a uranium gun-type bomb that exploded with about thirteen kilotons of force.
